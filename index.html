Intro: (What is Marketing?)

So to start off, Marketing is the process of determining the success of a new service or product through research done with possible customers. Doing so information like opinions and feedback are gathered. The four components of marketing are product, place, price, promotion. Such activities like public relations, sales promotion, advertising, social media, distribution and other functions. So, marketing isn’t just about a specific product, it’s a range.
